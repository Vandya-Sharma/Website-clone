BITS-RMIT Higher Education Academy admission offer withdrawal link (Posted on 20-JUL-2023)

Dear Aspirant,

Candidates who have accepted the offered BITS-RMIT Higher Education Academy admission by paying the requisite fees and now want to withdraw their candidature from the admission process can CLICK HERE to submit the withdrawal request.

Please note that once the withdrawal request is submitted, your name would be removed from the admission process and the deposited fees would be refunded as per clauses written in the offer letter. This process is irreversible, i.e. once the withdrawal request is submitted, your name would be removed from all the further iterations of the admission process.

Deadline for submitting the withdrawal request is 20-JUL-2023 (5:00 PM).
